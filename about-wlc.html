About WLC

about wlc

World Lethwei Championship is the definitive global sports media property for Lethwei. Founded in 2016, the Myanmar-based promotion has brought together the greatest fighters in the history of Lethwei and has the backing of the Burmese government to grow the sport of Lethwei on the international stage.

Combining a world-class event atmosphere with best martial arts strikers in the world, World Lethwei Championship delivers a breath taking experience for both fans in attendance and the millions watching at home.

Lethwei is the Burmese national martial arts for self-defence. History dates the martial arts back more than a millennium ago and were used in unarmed combat in several wars.

Lethwei is steeped in tradition and rituals are conducted before and after the fights. Traditional Burmese music is also played while the fight is taking place.

In Lethwei, hands, feet, knees, elbows and head are permitted to be used in the fight. The fights are judged by knockouts, where the referee will start counting once a competitor has been felled, or the bout will be decided by three judges.

World Lethwei Champion will win the World Lethwei Champion Belt. Weighing more than 6kg, the belt is gold and will be the most expensive belt ever made in lethwei history. The gold belt will be awarded to the best fighters in each weight class and a sliver belt will be awarded to the best local fighter in each weight class.

Zay Thiha is the chairman of Lekkha Moun Co,Ltd the parent company of World Lethwei Championship. With more than 20 years of experience spearheading the growth of Myanmar's leading brands in construction, IT, hospitality and sports, Mr Zay is well poised to drive the growth of the country's most revered combat sports.

The Board of Directors also include Co Sithu Steve, a pioneer in marketing in the country with more than two decades of experience. It also includes Ko Kyaw Aung, a top international businessman having led projects in multiple countries across Asia, and Ko Zarni Soe, managing director of construction company 3G Alliance.

Mingalardon Event Zone is an event space located in Mingalardon Township in Yangon, Myanmar. Specifically created for World Lethwei Championship, the arena can host up to 5,000 people and boasts state-of-the-art facilities like a dedicated VIP area, cashless transactions and more to provide fans the ideal event experience!

Mingalardon Event Zone is located within Royal Mingalardon Golf & Country Club on Khayae Pin Road, Minglardon Township, Yangon, Myanmar.
